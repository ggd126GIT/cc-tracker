The parties acknowledge that the Borrower obtained the amount of Seventy-Nine Thousand Pesos (₱79,000.00) from the Lender. The loan shall accrue simple interest at four percent (4%) per month, computed from the date of release until the date of this Agreement, covering a total period of twelve and four-tenths (12.4) months, as follows:
•	Monthly Interest: ₱79,000 × 4% = ₱3,160
•	Total Interest for 12.4 months: ₱3,160 × 12.4 = ₱39,184
•	Total Amount Due: ₱79,000 + ₱39,184 = ₱118,184
The Borrower made a partial payment of ₱3,000 on December 5, 2025, thereby reducing the total outstanding balance to ₱115,184, which amount the Borrower hereby expressly acknowledges and confirms.
WHEREAS, the BORROWER agrees to settle the total amount through monthly installments and to provide collateral. NOW THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the foregoing, both parties hereby agree as follows:
1.	LOAN AMOUNT 
The BORROWER acknowledges receipt of ₱79,000.00 from the LENDER. 
     2. INTEREST 
The loan shall earn five percent (4%) monthly interest, and accumulated interest for the 12-month non-payment period amounting to ₱39,184.00 is hereby recognized and accepted by the BORROWER.
     3. TOTAL AMOUNT DUE 
As of the execution of this Agreement, the BORROWER owes the LENDER the total amount of ONE HUNDRED FIFTEEN THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED EIGHTYFOUR PESOS (₱115,184.00).
     4. MODE OF PAYMENT 
The BORROWER shall pay the total amount of ₱115,184.00 in monthly installments of SEVEN THOUSAND PESOS (₱7,000.00) until fully paid. Payments shall be made on or before the 30th day of each month. 
     5. LATE PAYMENT PENALTY 
If the Borrower fails to pay for any monthly installment when due, the Borrower agrees that the following charges shall apply:
1.	A late payment penalty of one percent (1%) of the remaining unpaid balance as of the due date; and
2.	Penalty interest of five percent (5%) per month on the outstanding balance for the month in which the payment was missed.
     6. COLLATERAL
As security for this loan, the BORROWER hereby voluntarily mortgages/sanla her house (no land title) located at: ________________________________________________________________________
The BORROWER warrants that said property is: 
• Free from any claims, disputes, liens, or encumbrances. 
• Not subject to sanla tira, mortgage, prenda, or any third-party claim. 
In case of continued non-payment, the LENDER may enforce all remedies allowed by law against the collateral. 
     7. TERMINATION 
This Agreement shall terminate only upon full payment of the entire loan amount, including interest, penalties, and charges.
     8. VOLUNTARY EXECUTION
Both parties declare that they have read and understood this Agreement fully and voluntarily execute it without force, intimidation, or undue influence.
